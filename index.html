What Eva does

Three features.
That's the product.

Every feature solves a real moment in someone's day. No fluff. No setup. Just done.

Speak It
Standing at the school gate, one hand on the buggy — say the task out loud. Eva creates it, sets the reminder, reads it back. Done in 8 seconds.
Shift It
Kid is sick. One tap on "reschedule my day" and Eva moves everything non-urgent forward, flags the one hard deadline, and asks for confirmation.
Plan It
Type a goal — "run a 5K in 3 months, early mornings only." Eva builds a weekly plan, blocks the slots, and sets the first reminder. A wish becomes a Monday commitment.
Focus Mode
Day going sideways? One tap surfaces your top 3 priorities and defers everything else. Clarity in seconds when you need it most.
Quick List
Add groceries and errands hands-free while you cook, drive, or wrangle kids. Eva catches duplicates so your list stays clean.
Email Digest
Inbox summarised every morning. Urgent emails flagged. Draft replies ready for one-tap approval. Eva never sends a single email without you confirming.
How it works

Three steps to a calmer day.

01
Say it or type it
Speak a task hands-free on the school run, type it in 5 seconds at your desk, or tap the mic on your watch. Eva understands natural language — no commands needed.
02
Eva handles the rest
She schedules, reminds, reschedules around protected family time, and flags conflicts before they blow up your day. One tap to confirm — nothing happens without you.
03
Keep going
Eva recovers when days go sideways. Sick kid, overrun meeting, last-minute change — she adapts the plan so you don't have to rebuild it from scratch.
